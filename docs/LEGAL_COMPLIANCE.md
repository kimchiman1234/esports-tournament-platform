# Legal Compliance Guide

## Mandatory Disclaimers

All tournaments must display:

**"This competition is not affiliated with or sponsored by Riot Games, Inc."**

This disclaimer must appear on:
- Tournament creation page
- Tournament details page
- Payment confirmation screen
- Platform footer
- Terms & Conditions

## Skill-Based Competition Requirements

✅ **Allowed:**
- Entry fees converted to prize pools
- Outcome determined solely by player skill
- Match results verified via official Riot API
- Transparent fee breakdown

❌ **Prohibited:**
- Randomization in team assignment (must use MMR-based balancing)
- House betting or odds
- Casino-style mechanics
- Gambling language or mechanics

## Age & Location Verification

### Age Verification
- 18+ only
- Checkbox confirmation at signup
- Email verification required
- Consider additional verification for payouts >$600

### Geo-Blocking

## Payment Compliance

### KYC Requirements
- Required for payouts >$600
- Collect government ID
- Verify identity
- Store securely

### Tax Reporting
- Generate 1099 forms for eligible payouts
- Maintain transaction logs
- Monthly reporting capability

### Stripe Compliance
- Use Stripe's fraud detection
- Monitor chargeback rates
- Maintain PCI compliance
